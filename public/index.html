About Our Project

Heart Track is an innovative IoT-based health monitoring system developed as part of the ECE 413/513 course at the University of Arizona. Our project combines hardware sensors with a modern web application to provide real-time heart rate and blood oxygen (SpO2) monitoring.

The system features a Raspberry Pi microcontroller paired with a MAX30102 pulse oximeter sensor, enabling users to track their vital signs throughout the day. Data is securely transmitted to our cloud server and displayed through an intuitive dashboard accessible from any device.

Key capabilities include customizable measurement schedules, offline data storage, weekly health summaries, and a physician portal for healthcare providers to monitor their patients remotely.
